About TOMBARBAR

Guangzhou Bloom-win Fashion Co., Limited/H.K. Bloom-Win Corp. Limited is engaged in the developing, producing and selling leather fashion bags & belts.

TOMBARBAR is committed to the design, production and sales of environmentally fashion leather accessories. We not only create for our brand TAMBARBAR bags, but also offer OEM/ODM service for other brands. We have done OEM/ODM service for more than 12 years with fashion bags & belts. We have supplied to JOSEPH RIBKOFF, GERRY WEBER, OODJI, TOPSHOP, LOVE REPUBLIC, ZARINA, BEFREE, TOP SECRET, JCPENNY...... Making value to customers, creating value for employees, improving value for the products are the only and important aim for us to work hard.

Good understanding, high responsibility, deep passion on work helps us to win more an more trust from our customers.

Welcome to contact us for more information, and create cooperation!
